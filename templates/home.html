py-EDUCATOR



pyEducate is an online education platform where we plan to bring to every user the best available techniques to learn concepts and achieve the goals they set for themselves. We currently offer three products - a text summarizer that can summarize any text for you and show only the key details that will be relevant to you based on your choices, a mind map for all those who prefer to learn visually and finally a personalised question generation module that will generate relevant questions based on the document you have entered so that you can check how well you have learnt!
